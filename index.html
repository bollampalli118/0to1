Projection Formula

+ Surplus × 12 × N
+ MF Value × (1+r_mf)^N – MF₀
+ Gold Value × (1+r_gold)^N – Gold₀
+ Land Value × (1+r_land)^N × land_pct – Land₀
+ PF × (1+r_pf)^N – PF₀
+ LIC Bonus (if year ≥ LIC maturity year)
